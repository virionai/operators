# Product Narrative

Capsules.run Operators is an offline local-first investigative workspace with local Gemma reasoning and portable Capsule handoff. It makes context visible: evidence, event order, local inference, provenance, continuity, and handoff all remain inspectable inside the workspace.

For Gemma4Good/Kaggle, Gemma is positioned as resident cognition: it extracts, correlates, proposes, and drafts while the operator stays in control of the evidence surface. Live Gemma answers come from local Ollama when available; deterministic fallback answers keep the app functional without cloud access.

Capsules are positioned as transfer infrastructure: they preserve investigative continuity across disconnected machines and future reviewers. The current seal/export path creates a hashed local Capsule JSON handoff artifact; byte-compatible Capsule v0.6 packaging remains the next integration gate.

Competition positioning:

> A portable intelligence operating environment for local investigation and cryptographic collaboration.

The app should differentiate from chatbots, copilots, AI notebooks, and cloud orchestration dashboards by making the operational substrate visible.
