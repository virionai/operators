# PRODUCT

register: product

## Product Purpose

Capsules.run Operators is a local-first intelligence workspace for evidence-driven investigation, Gemma-assisted reasoning, and portable Capsule handoff. It opens as a blank operational surface, then lets the operator declare an environment, stage artifacts, generate workspace surfaces, and preserve continuity through local ledger events.

## Users

Security analysts, incident responders, investigators, and technical reviewers working in disconnected, sensitive, or high-trust environments. They need fast local cognition without losing control of evidence, provenance, and handoff boundaries.

## Tone

Quiet operational trust. The interface should feel like a forensic workstation and protocol instrument, not SaaS productivity software, cyberpunk scenery, or a chatbot.

## Strategic Principles

- Gemma is a resident inference layer, not the app itself.
- Context, provenance, topology, and continuity must be visible operational primitives.
- Workspace objects should feel malleable: generated, moved, selected, inspected, and rehydrated.
- Capsule handoff is a local continuity primitive until protocol-native packing is integrated.
- Blank state should activate the operator quickly through declaration and local artifact staging.

## Anti-References

Generic dashboards, landing pages, SIEM clutter, modal-heavy workflows, neon security tropes, chat-first AI products, and decorative UI controls without operational meaning.
